## Interpretation

Claude's research is aligned with Artur's monetization need.

The list is not mostly consumer/B2C. It is mostly:

- business diagnostics;
- operational audits;
- revenue/process leakage checks;
- service-led lead magnets;
- tools that can turn into consulting offers.

CV Reality Check AI is the exception, not the main direction.

It is useful as:

- a personal-story MVP;
- a visible public tool;
- an easy first launch;
- a portfolio artifact.

But the stronger money direction is B2B.
